What's the problem?

  • Sharing photos online is now easier than ever
  • Social media offers an easy insight into other people's lives, leading to an increased pressure to be 'perfect'
  • Most devices are now capable of complex photo edits
  • The amount of photo editing information on the internet is overwhelming

    • Why is this important?

  • The majority of communication done online is now done via photos (e.g. Instagram, Facebook)
  • Accessing only photography tutorials specific to beginners is difficult, meaning that the average user feels intimidated by the topic
  • Tutorials are often in inconvenient or outdated formats unsuitable for consumption on a mobile device

    • Why me?

  • Photography is a great passion of mine and has always been one of my main hobbies
  • I enjoy helping others and teaching new things
  • I'd like to learn iOS/MacOS app development and apply it to a practical, real-world scenario
  • I enjoyed the 'Image Processing' L2 submodule
